Crystal Collector

Instructions

At the start of each game we will provide you with a random numeral

Notice the four, beautiful crystals below. By clicking on one of these crystals you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You can win the game by matching your total score to the random numeral. You will lose the game if your total score goes above the random numeral.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you but does not change - you must click on them to discover their value.
